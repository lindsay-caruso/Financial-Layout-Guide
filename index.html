Real Estate Agent Fee Breakdown (Estimated)

Here's a breakdown of the typical costs real estate agents face on a monthly, quarterly, and yearly basis.

Monthly Expenses:

1. Brokerage Fees (Desk or Transaction Fees)

Estimated Cost: $85 to $150 per month

Details: These fees cover office space, administrative support, and other resources provided by the brokerage.

Monthly Equivalent: $85 to $150

2. Errors and Omissions (E&O) Insurance

Estimated Cost: $25 to $100 per month

Details: Protects against legal claims related to mistakes or omissions in your work.

Monthly Equivalent: $25 to $100

3. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Software

Estimated Cost: $85 to $100 per month

Details: Tools like Follow Up Boss help manage client relationships and automate follow-ups.

Monthly Equivalent: $85 to $100

By understanding these expenses, you can better plan and budget for a successful real estate career.
